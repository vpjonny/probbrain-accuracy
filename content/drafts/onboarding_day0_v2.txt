Welcome to ProbBrain.

Here's what we do and why it might be useful to you.

---

WHAT THIS IS

We monitor prediction markets — primarily Polymarket — and build our own probability estimates from the ground up. When the market price diverges meaningfully from our estimate, we post a signal.

Example of what a signal looks like:

  Russia-Ukraine ceasefire by end of 2026? (NO)
  Market: 34.5% YES  |  Our estimate: 19% YES
  Gap: +15.5% — NO appears underpriced

  Evidence:
  - US-brokered talks on hold — Washington focus shifted to Iran war
  - Russia demands Ukraine cede Donetsk — non-starter for Kyiv
  - Historical base rate: ~12% for territorial-dispute ceasefires
    within 9 months of active fighting

That last bullet — the base rate — is the core of our edge.

---

HOW WE ESTIMATE

Most market participants anchor on recent news. We start from historical base rates: how often has this type of event resolved YES, across comparable situations? Then we layer in current evidence that could push the probability above or below that base.

The result is a calibrated estimate, not a gut feeling. Sometimes we're wrong. We log those too.

---

WHAT TO EXPECT

- Signals when we see a genuine gap (not on a fixed schedule — if there's no edge, we don't send)
- The market price, our estimate, the gap, and the evidence behind it
- Resolution notes when markets close — what happened and what it means for our track record
