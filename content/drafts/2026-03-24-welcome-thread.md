## TWEET 2 (how signals work)

How a signal gets generated:

1. Identify the event category and its historical base rate
2. Layer in current evidence (news, data, expert assessments)
3. Generate a calibrated probability estimate
4. Compare to the market price
5. Signal fires only when the gap exceeds our threshold

Most markets we scan: no signal. The edge is knowing when to say nothing.
